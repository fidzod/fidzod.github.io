# Toby Jordan
*Self-Taught Programmer and Philosophy & RS Graduate*

Philosophy and Religious Studies from the University of Manchester, UK.

lambda calculus interpreter in Odin - read it
[here](/blog/lambda-calculus-interpreter-in-odin). In the next post, coming
soon, I will implement and discuss the *Simply Typed Lambda Calculus*.
